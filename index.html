Who We Are

LMT Agro, a sister concern of LMT CP., manages dairy and cattle farms using eco-friendly methods to deliver pure, nutritious dairy products without adulteration.

With a commitment to sustainability, the company sources 100% fresh milk straight from its herds, ensuring reliability and natural taste for daily nutrition.
